DigitalCharity

A transaction to make a change.
An NFT that funds charities.

When you buy a Charity NFT, you’re not simply buying an avatar or a provably-rare piece of art. You are gaining membership access to a club whose combined goal is to make a difference in today’s society through Charity. Your Charity NFT can serve as your digital identity, and open digital doors for you.

We compile a list of charity organisations for each NFT collection that are able to cooperate effectively in solving one particular issue of today’s society, which is determined by the collection. At the end of each month, 50% of the raised funds are split equally between the charities, and the remaining funds will be used for researching the best possible organisations to include in our collections and developing NFTs.

The Charity NFT collectibles will be a token of your charity contributions as well your digital identity that grants you access to charity discord channels.

Further information

The specs

Each NFT is unique and programmatically generated from over lots of possible traits, including expression, headgear, clothing, and some logo of the charity. All images are unique, but some are rarer than others. The charity NFTs are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. (See Record and Proof.) Purchasing one costs 0.1 ETH. To access members-only areas such as our Charity Discord channel, NFT holders will need to be signed in their Metamask Wallet.
