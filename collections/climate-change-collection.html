Climate change collection

Save the Earth before it's too late!

When you buy an NFT from this collection, you support charities that aim to raise awareness or counteract effects of the climate change.

The list of charities for this collection is the following:

Clean Air Task Force
Clean Air Task Force pushes the change in technologies and policies needed to get to a zero-emissions, high-energy planet at an affordable cost. They imagine a world where the energy needs of all people are met efficiently without damaging the atmosphere.
Carbon180
Carbon180 is a new breed of climate-focused NGO on a mission to fundamentally rethink carbon. They work with leading scientists, businesses, and policymakers to build a world that removes more carbon than it emits.
Climate Emergency Fund
Climate Emergency Fund funds young, ultra-ambitious organizations that tell the truth about the climate emergency, disrupt normalcy, organize mass protests, and demand transformative change.
Evergreen Action
Evergreen is leading the fight to put bold climate action at the top of America's agenda. Rainforest Foundation
Rainforest Foundation has a range of projects across Africa and South America, each focused on different aspects of rainforest protection and community land rights. Whether providing legal and technical training or supporting advocacy and rights awareness, all their projects are based on a participatory approach that puts local people at the heart of what they do.
